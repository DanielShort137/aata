Abstract Algebra: Theory and Applications

Everything you wanted to know about abstract algebra, but were afraid to buy

This text is free to download, but if you are doing more than just browsing, then purchasing a print copy is less expensive than printing it on a laser printer, less expensive than a single copy (unbound) at a copy shop, and very close to prices charged by copy shops when ordering multiple copies at a discount. Before ordering, you might want to examine an evaluation copy.

A nicely printed and bound (hardback) copy of AATA can be purchased from Amazon.com for roughly $19.95. This is a service provided by Lon Mitchell. The author, while supportive of this edition, is not involved directly in any way, including financially.

A note about editions: There have been some discrepancies in chapter numbering in earlier editions. The orginal version contained a Chapter 0, now numbering begins with Chapter 1. For the 2011 Edition, a chapter on normal subgroups and homomorphisms was split into two new chapters, affecting the numbering of later sections. The numbering of chapters should be consistent beginning with the 2011 Edition. The 2009 print version lacks some highlighted inline definitions.
